About Us

At Kemset, we are committed to shaping a sustainable future through innovative, environmentally responsible building products. As both a manufacturer and distributor, our mission is clear: to provide high-performance solutions that are safe for people, protective of the environment, and cost-effective throughout their lifecycle. Our products are carefully engineered to meet the highest standards of safety and sustainability. We prioritize materials that are non-toxic to workers and building occupants, recyclable, and free from harmful environmental impacts. With a strong focus on energy efficiency, durability, and ease of application, KemsetUSA products are designed to reduce energy consumption, minimize maintenance, and extend the lifespan of buildings. From reducing transportation costs with lighter, smarter materials to enhancing indoor air quality and occupant comfort, everything we do is driven by our core values—health, sustainability, innovation, and long-term value. At KemsetUSA, we don’t just build materials—we build better spaces for people to live, work, and thrive.
